PENTAGON

Definition

A pentagon is a five-sided polygon with five angles.

Properties

  • Has five sides and five angles.
  • The sum of the interior angles is 540°.
  • In a regular pentagon, all sides and all angles are equal.

Formulas

  • Area (for regular pentagon): A = (1/4)√(5(5 + 2√5)) * a²
  • Perimeter: P = 5a
