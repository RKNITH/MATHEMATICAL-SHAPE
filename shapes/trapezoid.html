TRAPEZOID

Definition

A trapezoid (or trapezium) is a quadrilateral with at least one pair of parallel sides. The parallel sides are called bases, and the non-parallel sides are called legs.

Properties

  • Has at least one pair of parallel sides.
  • The angles between a base and a leg are supplementary.
  • In an isosceles trapezoid, the non-parallel sides (legs) are equal in length, and the base angles are equal.

Formulas

  • Area: A = ((a + b) / 2) * h
  • Perimeter: P = a + b + c + d
