STRAIGHT LINE PROPERTIES AND CALCULATIONS

Definition

A straight line is the shortest distance between two points in a plane and extends infinitely in both directions.

Equation of a Line

  • Point-Slope Form: y - y1 = m(x - x1)
  • Two-Point Form: (y - y1) / (y2 - y1) = (x - x1) / (x2 - x1)
  • Slope-Intercept Form: y = mx + c
  • Intercept Form: x / a + y / b = 1
  • Normal Form: x cos θ + y sin θ = p
  • Parametric Form: x = x1 + r cos θ, y = y1 + r sin θ
