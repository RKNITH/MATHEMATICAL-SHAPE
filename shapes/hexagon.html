HEXAGON

Definition

A hexagon is a six-sided polygon with six angles.

Properties

  • Has six sides and six angles.
  • The sum of the interior angles is 720°.
  • In a regular hexagon, all sides and all angles are equal.

Formulas

  • Area (for regular hexagon): A = (3√3/2) * a²
  • Perimeter: P = 6a
