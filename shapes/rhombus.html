RHOMBUS

Definition

A rhombus is a quadrilateral whose four sides all have the same length. Another name for a rhombus is a diamond or a lozenge.

Properties

  • All sides are equal.
  • Opposite angles are equal.
  • Diagonals bisect each other at right angles.

Formulas

  • Area: A = (d1 * d2) / 2
  • Perimeter: P = 4a
