RECTANGLE

Definition

A rectangle is a quadrilateral where opposite sides are equal and all angles are right angles (90°).

Properties

  • Opposite sides are equal.
  • All angles are right angles (90°).
  • Diagonals are of equal length and bisect each other.

Formulas

  • Area: A = length × width
  • Perimeter: P = 2 × (length + width)
  • Diagonal: d = √(length² + width²)
