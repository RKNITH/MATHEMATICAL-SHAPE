SQUARE

Definition

A square is a regular quadrilateral where all four sides are equal in length and all angles are right angles (90°).

Properties

  • All sides are equal.
  • All angles are right angles (90°).
  • Diagonals are equal in length and bisect each other at right angles.

Formulas

  • Area: A = a²
  • Perimeter: P = 4a
  • Diagonal: d = a√2
